Global COVID-19 Access Tracker

The ACT-Accelerator and Multilateral Leaders Task Force on Scaling COVID-19 Tools Global COVID-19 Access Tracker is a dynamic tool to measure progress towards the global targets for access to COVID-19 vaccines, treatments, tests, and PPE, barriers in access to these COVID-19 tools, and delivery of donor pledges.

Vaccination

The vaccination targets are to achieve 40% coverage in all countries by the end-2021 and 70% by mid-2022. Vaccines are the most effective tool to prevent severe disease and death, and lower transmission.

Diagnotics & Testing

The diagnostics target is for all countries to achieve testing rates of 1 per 1,000 people per day by the end of 2021 / in 2022. Testing is needed to identify disease hotspots and track the emergence of new, dangerous variants.

Theraputics & Clinical Care

The therapeutics target is to treat all severe cases of COVID-19 / treat 200 million cases. Treatments, including medical oxygen, are needed for those severely affected by COVID-19 or to prevent those in at-risk groups from progressing to severe disease.

About Us

This website is part of the Health Systems & Response Connector (HSRC) of the Access to COVID-19 Tools (ACT) Accelerator. The goal of the HSRC is to ensure all countries have the necessary technical, operational and financial resources to translate new COVID-19 tools into effective national response interventions.

Australia
